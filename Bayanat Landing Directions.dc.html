The moat

The most valuable Arabic data can't leave the country.

Saudi Arabia's PDPL, enforced by SDAIA, makes exporting sensitive data a slow, risk-assessed, legally fraught process — with fines up to SAR 5M. A San Francisco vendor is, structurally, on the wrong side of the border.

We are in-Kingdom, in-house, and compliant by construction. That isn't a marketing claim — it's a legal fact that disqualifies our largest competitors from the workloads that matter most.
